# AI Discovery Workshop Playbook

## Objective
Identify high-value AI use cases for a client.

## Duration
2–3 hours

## Agenda

### 1. Introduction (15 mins)
- AI capabilities overview
- Responsible AI principles

### 2. Opportunity Identification (45 mins)
- Map business processes
- Identify pain points

### 3. Use Case Brainstorming (45 mins)
- Apply AI to pain points
- Capture ideas

### 4. Prioritisation (30 mins)
Score based on:
- Value
- Feasibility
- Risk

## Outputs
- Prioritised use case list
- Initial backlog

## Tools
- Miro / Whiteboard
- This repo’s use-case templates
